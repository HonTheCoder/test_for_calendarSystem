    Scheduling Policies
    Please read before submitting your request
    01
    24-Hour Allowed Cancellation. After submitting, you may cancel this request within 24 hours without admin approval — just go to My Meetings and click Cancel. After 24 hours, a cancellation request will be sent to the admin for review.
    02
    Admin Approval Required. Your meeting will stay as Pending until the admin approves or rejects it. You will be notified of the decision.
    03
    Weekdays Only, Office Hours. Meetings must be on Monday–Friday, between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM. No bookings on Philippine public holidays.
    04
    One Slot per Time Block. Overlapping pending requests may be auto-cancelled if another meeting for the same time slot gets approved first.
